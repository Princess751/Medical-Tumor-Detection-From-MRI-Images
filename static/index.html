    System architecture

    Model: ResNet-18 fine-tuned for 4-class MRI classification (glioma, meningioma, pituitary, no tumor).
    Preprocessing: CLAHE contrast enhancement, resize to 224×224, ImageNet normalization.
    Explainability: Grad-CAM on layer4 highlights regions influencing the prediction.
    Storage: SQLite audit log of studies and actions (local only).

    Known limitations

    • Single 2D slice analysis — does not replace full volumetric review or multi-sequence MRI.
    • Performance depends on scanner, sequence, and patient population; requires local validation.
    • Grad-CAM shows correlation with model weights, not histologically confirmed tumor extent.
    • Synthetic demo weights must be replaced with hospital-authorized training data for real deployment.
